# AI-Native News Experience — Impact Model (Prototype)

## Assumptions
- Daily active users in pilot: **10,000**
- Average business-news reading sessions/user/day: **2**
- Average stories opened/session today: **4**
- Time per story in legacy format: **4 min**
- Time to useful understanding with decision briefing flow: **2.5 min**
- Subscription upgrade conversion uplift from personalization and clarity: **+1.2% absolute**

## 1) Time Saved
Baseline time/day:
- 10,000 users × 2 sessions × 4 stories × 4 min = **320,000 min/day**

Decision-briefing time/day:
- 10,000 × 2 × 4 × 2.5 = **200,000 min/day**

**Time saved = 120,000 min/day (2,000 hours/day)**

## 2) Engagement Improvement (Proxy)
If decision-focused presentation reduces cognitive load:
- Assume +18% higher session completion for high-intent users
- Assume higher repeat usage because users can get to “what changed for me?” faster

## 3) Revenue Recovery/Uplift (Illustrative)
If 1.2% of 10,000 users convert due to better personalization and trust:
- Incremental conversions/day = 120 users
- If average effective monthly value/user = ₹300
- Monthly uplift (cohort basis) ≈ **₹36,000/month** from one daily cohort

## 4) Editorial Efficiency (Future)
With reusable briefing generation around major story clusters:
- Assume newsroom saves 15 min per major story packet
- 40 packets/day → 600 min/day saved (**10 editorial hours/day**)

## 5) Why Judges Should Care
- The user-side time impact is immediate and measurable.
- Explainable personalization increases trust compared with black-box feeds.
- The architecture can evolve into a real intelligence layer, not just a summary widget.
